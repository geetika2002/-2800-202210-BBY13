Green Seal Certification

Our science-based standards define sustainability leadership in the marketplace and reward industry innovations. Only the healthiest, most responsible, and most effective products achieve the Green Seal.

Asthma & Allergy Friendly

When you see the asthma & allergy friendly® Certification Mark you know that the product has been scientifically tested and proven to be more suitable for people with asthma and related allergic sensitivities.

The asthma & allergy friendly® Certification Program was developed and launched in Canada by Allergy Standards Limited (ASL) in partnership with Asthma Canada (AC).

The Certification Standards are applied to products to ensure that they fulfill suitability criteria for individuals with asthma and allergic sensitivities.
